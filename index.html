Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need prior experience to join?

No. Each course starts from the fundamentals and builds up gradually, with guided practice along the way.

How much time should I set aside each week?

Most learners spend about 2–3 hours per week across four weeks, though you can move at your own pace.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes. Every learner who completes the roadmap receives a certificate of completion.

What happens if a course is "Coming Soon"?

You can still sign up early. You'll be added to the priority list and notified as soon as seats open.

Can I switch courses after signing up?

Yes, just mention it in the message field on the sign-up form and our team will follow up by email.
